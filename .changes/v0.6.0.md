## v0.6.0

v0.6.0 makes badge-award filtering more direct and removes a GraphQL filter shape that looked valid but could not actually run.

### Added

- Badge award queries now support a `badgeType` filter. Clients can fetch `app.certified.badge.award` records by the type of the referenced badge definition without joining badge definitions on the client.

### Fixed

- Nested-array filters no longer advertise unsupported `any` scopes inside another array `any` scope. Those filters passed GraphQL validation but failed at execution. Nested arrays still support `isNull` checks, and regular single-array `any` filters are unchanged.
